# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 19, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to Cheezus ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Email address** - Used for account creation, authentication, and important communications
- **Display name** - Your chosen username visible to other users
- **Profile photo** - Optional profile image you upload

### Cheese Activity Data
- **Cheese logs** - Cheeses you add to your cheese box, including ratings, notes, and review dates
- **Wishlist items** - Cheeses you save to try later
- **Flavor preferences** - Your taste preferences and flavor tags
- **Photos** - Images you upload of cheeses and labels

### Social Data
- **Following/Followers** - Users you follow and who follow you
- **Badges and achievements** - Progress and accomplishments within the app

### Device Information
- **Push notification tokens** - To send you notifications (if enabled)
- **Device type** - For app optimization and support

### AI Label Scanner
When you use our label scanning feature:
- **Photos of cheese labels** - Temporarily processed to extract cheese information
- Label images are sent to OpenAI's Vision API for analysis
- Images are not stored permanently after processing

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- **Provide the service** - Enable you to log cheeses, track your cheese journey, and discover new cheeses
- **Social features** - Allow you to follow friends, see their activity, and share recommendations
- **Personalization** - Suggest cheeses based on your preferences and history
- **Notifications** - Send updates about your activity, badges earned, and friend activities (configurable)
- **Improve the app** - Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix issues
- **Support** - Respond to your questions and provide assistance

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell your personal data. We may share data with:

- **Supabase** - Our database and authentication provider (stores your account and activity data)
- **OpenAI** - Processes label images for the AI scanner feature (images are not retained)
- **Expo/Apple/Google** - For push notification delivery
- **Legal requirements** - If required by law or to protect our rights

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure
- We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest
- Account passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- We implement access controls to limit data access to necessary personnel

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** - Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correct** - Update inaccurate information in your profile
- **Delete** - Request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Export** - Download your cheese logs and activity data
- **Opt-out** - Disable notifications and certain data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us at [your-email@example.com].

## Data Retention

- **Active accounts** - Data retained while your account is active
- **Deleted accounts** - Personal data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Anonymized data** - Aggregated, non-identifiable data may be retained for analytics

## Children's Privacy

Cheezus is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Notification Settings

You can control notifications in the app:
- Push notifications (on/off)
- In-app notifications by type
- Email communications
- Quiet hours settings

## Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to external websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.
